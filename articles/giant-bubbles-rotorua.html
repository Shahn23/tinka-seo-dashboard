Best Places for Giant Bubbles in Rotorua

Kuirau Park

Rotorua's only free geothermal park is also one of its best bubble-blowing locations. The northern end of Kuirau Park has a large grassy area where kids can run and chase bubbles. The natural hot pools and steaming vents in the background make for an unforgettable bubble photo backdrop. Parking is free, and there's a children's playground right next door.

Government Gardens

Easily the most picturesque spot for giant bubbles in Rotorua. The manicured lawns in front of the historic Blue Baths building provide the perfect open space. The flower gardens add colour to your bubble photos, and the lake breeze from Lake Rotorua helps carry your bubbles for impressive distances. Located right on the lakefront, this is a must-visit for any bubble enthusiast.

Lake Rotorua Foreshore

The entire eastern edge of Lake Rotorua is a walking and cycling path with grassy areas at regular intervals. Pop down to the foreshore near the Rotorua Lakefront playground (next to the i-SITE visitor centre) for a perfect afternoon of bubbles by the water. The breeze off the lake is ideal for creating massive bubbles that drift over the water - a spectacular sight.

Sulphur Point Reserve

This lesser-known gem on the lake's edge offers wide-open spaces and stunning sunset views over Mokoia Island. The reserve's flat, grassy terrain is ideal for setting up bubble gear. It's quieter than the main lakefront, making it perfect for a relaxed family afternoon. Keep an eye out for the resident black swans - they seem to enjoy the bubbles too!

Redwoods Forest / Whakarewarewa Forest

For something truly unique, head to the Redwoods. The forest clearings and picnic areas are excellent for bubble play. Watching iridescent bubbles drift through the towering California Redwoods is a magical experience you won't forget. There are several picnic spots near the mountain bike trailhead that work perfectly.

How to Make Giant Bubbles in Rotorua - Step by Step

Getting started with giant bubbles is surprisingly simple. Here's everything you need:

What You'll Need

  • A quality giant bubble kit - The Tinka Giant Bubble Kit comes with everything you need: a professional wand designed for massive bubbles and concentrated bubble solution that outperforms anything from the supermarket
  • Your giant bubble solution - Mixed according to the instructions (trust us, it matters)
  • Calm or light breeze - Rotorua's lake breezes are perfect; avoid very windy days
  • Comfortable shoes - You'll be walking backward as your bubble inflates!

Technique for Maximum Bubbles

  1. Dip the wand fully into your bubble solution - let it soak for 10-15 seconds so the strings are fully saturated
  2. Lift the wand gently - let excess solution drip off for a moment (don't shake it!)
  3. Walk backward slowly while keeping the wand facing the wind - the bubble will inflate naturally
  4. Close the wand handles together gently to release the bubble - or give a gentle flick for an extra boost
  5. Watch your giant bubble float across Kuirau Park or Government Gardens!

Pro tip from Tinka: The secret to truly massive bubbles is patience. Let the bubble form slowly - rushing the release is the #1 reason bubbles pop prematurely. Rotorua's light lake breezes are your friend here.
